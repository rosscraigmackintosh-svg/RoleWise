# Audience
Rolewise is for people who want to make better decisions about roles.
It is especially useful when the cost of a bad fit is high.
## Primary audience
The core audience is thoughtful applicants who do not want to apply blindly.
They typically care about:
- role shape
- scope and ownership
- fit beyond title matching
- long-term direction
- avoiding low-signal hiring processes
## Strong-fit users
Rolewise is particularly well suited to:
- senior professionals
- contractors and consultants
- experienced product, design, and technology candidates
- people navigating ambiguous or high-variance roles
- users who have been burned by poor-fit hiring processes before
## Why these users fit well
These users tend to care less about volume and more about judgement.
They are less interested in seeing 500 roles and more interested in understanding whether a specific role is worth real attention.
## Broader audience
Rolewise is not limited to senior users.
Anyone can benefit from clearer role interpretation. But the product is designed around senior-grade judgement:
- trade-offs over simplification
- clarity over hype
- honesty over encouragement
## Users it is not optimised for
Rolewise is not optimised for people who want:
- high-volume job application workflows
- constant job-feed browsing
- instant "yes/no" verdicts
- motivational career coaching
## In one sentence
Rolewise is for thoughtful applicants who care more about decision quality than application volume.
