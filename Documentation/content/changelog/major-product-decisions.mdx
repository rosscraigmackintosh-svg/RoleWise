# Major product decisions
Some decisions shape the product deeply enough that they should remain visible.
## Examples
- No scoring doctrine
- No prediction stance
- Coding expectation rule (production = hard no)
- Location interpretation rules
- Outcome interpretation rules
## Why this matters
These decisions define:
- what Rolewise is
- what it refuses to become
- how it behaves over time
They should not be buried or forgotten.
## Rule
If a decision changes how the system behaves, record it.
## In one sentence
Major decisions define the product's boundaries and should remain visible.
