# Role Timeline
The Role Timeline shows what has happened for a specific role over time.
It tracks the journey from first reading a role through to its outcome.
## Why it exists
Job tracking is often fragmented. Events like reading a role, applying, receiving emails, and attending interviews are rarely connected. Role Timeline brings them together.
## What it does
For each role, the timeline records:
- when the role was first analysed
- when you applied (if you did)
- interview stages
- messages or updates
- final outcome

This creates a single, continuous view of the process.
## How it behaves
### Simple and chronological
Events appear in order. No optimisation or interpretation.

### Flexible structure
The timeline adapts to different processes — one interview, multiple stages, or no response.

### Lightweight updates
Events add quickly. The goal is capture reality, not admin work.
## What it is not
Role Timeline is not:
- a pipeline manager
- a performance tracker
- a structured hiring workflow tool

No enforced stages or process.
## How it fits into the product
- Applicant Mode starts the role
- Decision Snapshot records the decision
- Timeline tracks what happens next
- Weekly Review reflects on outcomes

## In one sentence
Role Timeline shows what actually happened for a role over time.
