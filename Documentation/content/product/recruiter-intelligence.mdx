# Recruiter Intelligence
Recruiter Intelligence captures information about the people sending you roles.
It helps you understand how they operate over time.
## Why it exists
Recruiter behaviour varies widely. Some send relevant roles, some generic outreach, some useful context, some nothing. Without tracking, patterns are invisible.
## What it does
Rolewise stores and surfaces:
- recruiter identity
- company or agency
- roles they send
- response patterns
- outcomes of their roles

This builds a clearer picture over time.
## How it behaves
### Descriptive, not judgemental
Shows patterns. No ranking or scoring.

### Built from real interactions
Insights from actual roles and outcomes, not assumptions.

### Long-term view
Value grows with each interaction recorded.
## What it is not
Recruiter Intelligence is not:
- a rating system
- a blacklist or whitelist
- a performance tool

No judgment of individuals.
## How it fits into the product
- Inbox captures recruiter data
- Roles link to recruiters
- Outcomes build patterns
- Weekly Review surfaces insights

## In one sentence
Recruiter Intelligence helps you understand who is sending roles and how they behave over time.
