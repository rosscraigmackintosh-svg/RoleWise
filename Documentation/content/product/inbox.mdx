# Inbox
The Inbox allows you to paste recruiter emails into Rolewise.
It extracts key information and connects it to your roles.
## Why it exists
Many roles come through email, not job boards. These messages contain role details, recruiter information, links, and process signals — but are unstructured and easy to lose.
## What it does
When you paste an email, Rolewise:
- identifies the role and company
- extracts key details
- detects recruiter information
- links it to an existing role or creates a new one

This turns email into structured data.
## How it behaves
### Non-invasive
You choose what to paste. No inbox access.

### Structured output
Extracts useful signals without over-processing. Clarity over completeness.

### Optional use
Works with or without Inbox.
## What it is not
Inbox is not:
- an email client
- a messaging system
- an automation tool

No email sending or management.
## How it fits into the product
- Email → Inbox
- Inbox → Role created or updated
- Role → analysed in Applicant Mode

## In one sentence
Inbox turns recruiter emails into structured role data.
