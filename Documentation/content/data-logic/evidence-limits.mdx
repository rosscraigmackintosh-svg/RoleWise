# Evidence limits
Rolewise has limits.
## What it cannot know
It cannot reliably determine:
- company culture
- leadership quality
- internal politics
- budget flexibility
- team dynamics
## Product rule
Where evidence is weak:
- say it clearly
- do not guess
- do not smooth uncertainty
## Why this matters
False certainty damages trust more than uncertainty.
## In one sentence
Limits should be visible, not hidden.
