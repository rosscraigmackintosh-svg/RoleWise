# Aggregated patterns
Rolewise becomes more useful as patterns emerge.
## What patterns are
Patterns are repeated signals across roles, such as:
- recurring blockers
- repeated rejection types
- common role shapes
- consistent mismatches
## Why patterns matter
Single roles can mislead.
Patterns reveal reality.
## Pattern discipline
Patterns should:
- be based on repeated signals
- avoid overfitting to small samples
- remain descriptive, not predictive
## In one sentence
Patterns turn repeated experience into usable understanding.
