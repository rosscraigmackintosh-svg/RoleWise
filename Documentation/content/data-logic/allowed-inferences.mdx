# Allowed inferences
Inference is useful when controlled.
## What Rolewise can infer
From a job description, Rolewise may infer:
- ownership level
- role scope
- clarity vs ambiguity
- delivery pressure
- structural gaps
## What it cannot infer
It should not infer:
- hidden culture truths
- hiring intent certainty
- internal team quality
## Rule
Inference should be:
- grounded
- explainable
- limited
## In one sentence
Inference should extend clarity, not invent reality.
