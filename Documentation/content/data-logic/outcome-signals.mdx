# Outcome signals
Outcomes are signals, not conclusions.
## What counts as a signal
Outcome signals include:
- response received
- interview stage reached
- rejection timing
- offer received
- no response after time
## Signal quality
Not all signals are equal.
A quick automated rejection is not the same as a multi-stage interview process.
## Interpretation rule
Signals should be interpreted in context:
- how strong was the evaluation?
- how much interaction occurred?
- was the process meaningful?
## Over time
Single outcomes are weak.
Patterns across multiple roles are useful.
## In one sentence
Outcome signals matter over time, not in isolation.
