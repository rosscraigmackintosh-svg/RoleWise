# Weekly review template
Use this to reflect on the last week without turning your search into a dashboard.
## Structure
- Roles seen
- Roles analysed
- Roles applied
- Roles skipped
- Responses received
- Interviews
- Rejections
- No responses
## Patterns noticed
- Repeated blockers
- Role types showing up
- Response quality
- Process patterns
## Questions
- What felt like a strong fit?
- What was clearly not worth pursuing?
- What felt unclear or misleading?
## One action
Choose one small action for next week.
## In one sentence
Keep it short, structured, and honest.
