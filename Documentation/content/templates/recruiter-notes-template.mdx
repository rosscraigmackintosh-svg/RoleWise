# Recruiter notes template
Keep recruiter notes simple and factual.
## Structure
- Recruiter name
- Company or agency
- Role sent
- Hiring company
## Interaction
- Date contacted
- Response speed
- Follow-ups
## Outcome
- Stage reached
- Final outcome
## Notes
Short, factual observations only.
## In one sentence
Track behaviour, not opinions.
