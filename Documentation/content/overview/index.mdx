# Overview
Rolewise is a decision-support tool that helps you decide whether a role is worth pursuing before you apply.
This manual explains how it works.
If you are new to Rolewise, start here.
## The core idea
Most job tools increase activity. Rolewise slows you down to understand roles clearly and decide what's worth your time.
---
## The system in one view
Rolewise works as a simple loop:

```
Radar → Applicant Mode → Decision → Snapshot → Weekly Review
↘ Role DNA ↗
```

- **Radar** → surfaces roles
- **Applicant Mode** → helps you understand them
- **Decision** → apply, skip, or watch
- **Decision Snapshot** → records your reasoning
- **Weekly Review** → helps you reflect over time

Underneath, **Role DNA** provides consistent role interpretation. Together: clearer decisions.

## What to expect
Rolewise is designed to be:
- clear rather than encouraging
- descriptive rather than predictive
- calm rather than urgent

Limited information is stated. Trade-offs are visible. The goal: better decisions, not faster ones.
