# What is Rolewise
Rolewise is a decision-support tool for job seekers. Most job platforms maximise activity. Rolewise helps you understand a role clearly, assess trade-offs, and decide if it's worth your time.
## Why it exists
Job descriptions are often unclear, inflated, or incomplete. Important signals are missing or softened. Rolewise slows that moment down — turning a job description into a structured view so you can decide clearly before applying.
## What it does
Rolewise helps the user:
- understand the likely shape of a role
- identify blockers, risks, and trade-offs
- spot vague or missing information
- decide whether to apply, skip, or keep watching
- build a clearer picture of what works over time

It supports judgment, not replacing it.
## Who it is for
Rolewise is for people wanting better role decisions — especially experienced professionals, contractors, and thoughtful applicants who avoid blind applications. It's built around senior-grade decision quality: clear trade-offs, calm language, honest interpretation.
## What makes it different
Rolewise is not a job board, ATS, or scoring tool. It doesn't maximise volume, rank you, predict outcomes, or create false confidence. It focuses on one question:
> Is this role worth pursuing?

It sits at the product's centre.
## How it behaves
Rolewise is clear rather than encouraging. Weak evidence is stated. Vague roles surface. Trade-offs become visible. It helps you think, not push forward.
## In one sentence
Rolewise helps people decide whether a role is worth pursuing before they apply.
