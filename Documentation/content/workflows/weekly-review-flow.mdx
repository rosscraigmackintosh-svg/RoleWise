# Weekly review flow
The weekly review flow turns activity into reflection.
## Flow

Roles → Decisions → Outcomes → Weekly Review → Patterns → One action

## What happens
- recent roles are collected
- decisions are grouped
- outcomes are summarised
- patterns are surfaced
## Behaviour
### Calm summary
No dashboards, no noise.
---
### Pattern-first
Focus on repeated signals, not individual events.
---
### One action
End with a single clear next step.
## Product goal
Shift from reactive behaviour to structured understanding.
## In one sentence
Weekly review connects activity to insight.
