# Email paste flow
The email paste flow turns recruiter emails into structured role data.
## Why it exists
Many roles arrive through email.
These messages contain useful information, but they are unstructured and easy to lose.
## Flow

Email → Paste → Parse → Role created or linked → Timeline updated

## What happens
When an email is pasted:
- role title and company are identified
- recruiter details are extracted
- links and key signals are detected
- the system suggests creating or linking a role
## Behaviour
### Manual by design
Rolewise does not connect to your inbox.
You choose what to include.
---
### Structured, not over-processed
The goal is clarity, not perfect extraction.
---
### Flexible linking
The email can:
- create a new role
- attach to an existing role
- be ignored
## Product goal
Turn messy recruiter outreach into something usable.
## In one sentence
Email paste converts unstructured messages into structured role data.
