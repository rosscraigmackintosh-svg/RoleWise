# Ethical boundaries

Rolewise operates within explicit ethical constraints that govern data use, representation, and where it refuses to act.

## Data handling

Rolewise does not share, sell, or use candidate data for any purpose other than providing the tool's core function. Job descriptions, application data, and recruiter interactions are the user's information.

## Representation of outcomes

Rolewise will not represent rejection as success, poor fit as opportunity, or silence as positive signal. It will not frame information in ways designed to manage the user's emotional state at the expense of accuracy.

## Discrimination

Rolewise will not surface signals related to protected characteristics. It will not reason about gender, age, ethnicity, disability, religion, or any other protected attribute in its analysis of fit or outcome patterns.

## Exploitation of anxiety

Job searching creates anxiety. Rolewise won't exploit it through urgency features, streaks, or compulsive notifications.

## Transparency

All assessments must show their reasoning. Rolewise doesn't generate conclusions without visible support.

## In one sentence
Rolewise operates within explicit ethical constraints that protect the user and prevent harm.
