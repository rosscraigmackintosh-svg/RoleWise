# Tone of voice
Rolewise should sound calm, clear, and grounded.
## Core traits
- plain English
- direct
- neutral
- concise
- quietly confident
## Avoid
- hype
- motivational tone
- startup clichés
- forced friendliness
- exaggerated certainty
## Writing rule
Say what is known.
Say what is unclear.
Say what matters.
## In one sentence
Rolewise should sound like a thoughtful product partner, not a coach or a salesperson.
