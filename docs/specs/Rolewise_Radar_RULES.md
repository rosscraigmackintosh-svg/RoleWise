## 1. Rolewise Radar RulesStatus: Active

Purpose:

Define how Radar summarizes and what it is allowed to infer.

---

Data Sources Radar uses

- Rolewise_Applications_Log.md

- Rolewise_Stats.md

- Rolewise_Recruiter_Intelligence.md

- Rolewise_Weekly_Review.md

- Rolewise_Decision_Patterns.md

- Rolewise_Applicant_Mode_RULES.md + Addendum

---

Non-negotiables

- Calm, short, factual.

- No motivational tone.

- No ranking of companies or “best role” claims.

- No speculation about intent or psychology.

- If data is missing, say “Not enough data yet”.

---

Time windows

- Rolling view defaults to last 14 days.

- Weekly view uses Monday to Sunday in Europe/London time.

---

Definitions

Stale:

- Applied or in-progress role with no response after 14 days.

Ghosted:

- Applied role with no response after 21 days.

Hot:

- Any role where response arrived within 3 days, or interview scheduled, or clear two-way comms are active.

Response time:

- Days between Applied date and first response date.

Salary disclosed:

- A role is “disclosed” only if a salary or range is explicitly stated in the listing or comms.

- If range exists, always show monthly equivalents.

Hard No:

- Any role requiring production-level coding is a hard no.

Acceptable:

- Prototyping or exploratory “vibe coding” is acceptable.

---

Radar outputs

Radar must always produce:

1. Activity counts2) Pipeline health3) Response intelligence4) Role mix signals5) Risk flags6) One small next action---## One small next action ruleOnly suggest 1 action.Make it simple.Examples:- “Follow up on the 3 roles past 14 days.”- “Prioritise roles with salary disclosed this week.”- “Stop spending time on 3+ days onsite roles.”
