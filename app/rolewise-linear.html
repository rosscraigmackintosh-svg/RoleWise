For the Stripe screening, I'd prioritise these three:

1. Coding expectations
"The JD mentions technical collaboration — can you help me understand where the PM role ends and engineering begins day-to-day?"

2. Hybrid flexibility
"The listing mentions 3 days on-site minimum. Is that fixed or is there flexibility on weeks with travel?"

3. Roadmap ownership
"Who are the key stakeholders I'd align with on roadmap decisions, and what does that relationship look like in practice?"
