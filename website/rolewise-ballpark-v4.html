Senior Product Manager
Operator-Builder Hybrid · Payments & Growth
Overview Role Summary

A senior individual contributor PM role focused on Stripe's payments product in London. Scope centres on merchant-facing product — improving conversion, reducing friction in checkout flows, and iterating on the core payments API experience.

This role exists because Stripe is expanding European PM capacity as merchant adoption grows in the region. The hire is likely to own a defined payment vertical rather than a generalist PM function, which is a positive scope signal.

Fit Reality How Well You Match
Strong product ownership expected — full roadmap accountability with direct stakeholder alignment, not execution-layer PM work.
"owns the end-to-end product roadmap for your domain, from ideation through delivery"
Technical depth required but scope is undefined — coding collaboration is mentioned without a clear boundary between PM and engineering ownership.
"partner closely with engineering on technical decisions and trade-offs"
Senior IC positioning is appropriate — no people management expectation stated, scope aligns with 6–8 years of experience, not a stretch.
"individual contributor role with significant cross-functional influence across the London team"
Fintech domain experience implied — payments-specific language throughout, though not explicitly required as a hard prerequisite.
"experience in payments, fintech, or complex transactional products preferred"
Day to Day What You'd Actually Do
Define and own the product roadmap for a defined payment vertical from ideation through delivery.
"own the roadmap for your product area, prioritising ruthlessly based on merchant impact"
Lead cross-functional alignment across engineering, design, data science, legal, and compliance.
"working closely with engineering, design, and compliance to ship iteratively"
Conduct merchant research to identify friction points and surface actionable opportunity areas.
"gathering feedback from merchants and converting qualitative insights into clear requirements"
Drive measurable improvements to conversion-critical flows using data-driven prioritisation.
"improving conversion and reducing drop-off across key payment flows with measurable outcomes"
Requirements What They're Looking For
6+ years PM experience — ideally in fintech, payments, or a high-volume transactional product context.
"6+ years of product management experience, preferably in payments or high-volume B2B SaaS"
Comfort working alongside world-class engineers — ability to engage credibly on technical decisions without being the decision-maker.
"ability to collaborate with and influence a world-class engineering team on technical direction"
Strong analytical and data mindset — evidence of using quantitative data to drive product prioritisation at scale.
"data-driven approach to prioritisation, comfortable with SQL and dashboarding tools"
Watch Out Friction Signals
⚠ Hybrid terms unconfirmed ⚠ Coding scope undefined ✕ No equity details stated ✓ Salary transparent ✓ Clear scope, no people mgmt
